🌿 Understanding NPK
N — Nitrogen

Drives leaf and stem growth. Low N = yellowing leaves. Sources: Urea (46%N), Ammonium Sulfate (21%N).

P — Phosphorus

Root & flower development. Low P = purple leaves. Sources: DAP (46%P), SSP (16%P).

K — Potassium

Disease resistance & fruit quality. Low K = brown leaf edges. Sources: MOP (60%K).
